FAQs

What is Kaamyaab?

Kaamyaab is an innovative career platform designed specifically for students, offering a seamless way to apply for internships, jobs, and research assistantships. It combines one-click applications with a curated job board and integrated career services to streamline your path to professional success.

How does it help me?

Kaamyaab simplifies your job search by providing tailored opportunities that match your academic background and career goals. With features like one-click applications, real-time updates, and access to vetted job listings, Kaamyaab helps you stay ahead in your career journey and increases your chances of getting hired quickly.

How can I get access?

To gain access to Kaamyaab, simply enter your .edu email below to join the waitlist. The more friends you refer to join the waitlist, the faster you can get early access to our platform. Share your unique referral link with fellow students, and for each successful sign-up, you'll move up the waitlist, getting you closer to unlocking Kaamyaab's powerful career tools sooner.
